About Us

Welcome to GreenLeaf Bistro, where passion for food meets exceptional service! Founded in 2010 in the heart of New York City, GreenLeaf Bistro was born out of a vision to offer fresh, wholesome meals in a cozy and welcoming environment. What started as a small, family-owned restaurant has grown into a thriving chain, with over 15 locations across the city and beyond.

At GreenLeaf Bistro, we take pride in every dish we create. Our chefs handpick the finest ingredients, ensuring each meal is made with care, love, and the utmost attention to detail. Whether it's a classic comfort dish or a seasonal special, we believe that great food starts with the best quality produce and the passion to make it perfect.

We are committed to offering a dining experience that reflects our values: quality, sustainability, and community. Every plate is crafted with the same dedication to excellence, whether it's your first visit or your hundredth. Join us at any of our locations and discover what makes GreenLeaf Bistro a place you’ll return to again and again.

Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond the kitchen. We actively support local farmers and sustainable practices, ensuring that our ingredients are not only fresh but also responsibly sourced. From farm to table, we strive to reduce our environmental footprint while providing you with the highest quality meals. GreenLeaf Bistro is more than just a place to eat – it's a community dedicated to nourishing both the body and the planet.

Seasonal Menu

Welcome to our menu! Here you’ll find a selection of our finest dishes, made with the freshest ingredients available.

  • Shrimp & Vegetable Stir-fry with Rice:

    A vibrant and healthy dish combining tender shrimp, crisp vegetables, and perfectly cooked rice. The shrimp is lightly seasoned and sautéed to perfection, paired with a colorful mix of fresh vegetables like bell peppers, zucchini, and carrots. All of this is served atop a bed of fluffy, steamed rice, creating a delicious balance of flavors and textures.

  • Grilled Steak & Fish with Vegetables and Rice:

    A hearty and flavorful dish featuring a perfectly grilled steak and a tender piece of fish, served alongside a medley of fresh, sautéed vegetables. The steak is seasoned to enhance its natural richness, while the fish is delicately cooked to achieve a light and flaky texture.

  • Turkey & Duck with Vegetables, Fruits, and Sauces:

    A luxurious and savory dish featuring tender turkey and succulent duck, perfectly cooked and complemented by a vibrant assortment of fresh vegetables and sweet, seasonal fruits. The rich flavors of the poultry are enhanced by a selection of gourmet sauces, adding depth and complexity to every bite.

  • Veggie Patties with Olives, Vegetables, and Sauce:

    A delicious and wholesome dish featuring crispy, golden veggie patties made from a mix of fresh, seasonal vegetables. The patties are complemented by briny olives, adding a burst of flavor to the dish.
